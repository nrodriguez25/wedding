Some Boston Ideas

Coming to Boston

Accommodations

We love Airbnb, and recommend it as a great option for your weekend in Boston (the Back Bay neighborhood is ideal)

The Westin is a highly convenient hotel option in the Back Bay neighborhood (shoutout to all you Starwood folks)

The Newbury Guest House is a quaint hotel right in the middle of the best part of the city (and our neighborhood)



Getting Around

For those flying into the Boston Airport, the Logan Express Back Bay is the easiest option to get into Back Bay. Our goal is for everyone to be able to get around without a rental car during your time here. Here are some of our favorite ways!

The “T” is Boston’s subway system, and is an easy way to get around to most places you’d like to go within the city

Lyft, well, gives you a lift when you aren’t in the mood to walk or take the T and is highly prevalent in Boston

Hubway is Boston’s bikeshare program, and has inexpensive day passes for taking rides around the city
